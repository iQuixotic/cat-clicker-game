Crystal Collector !!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are 5 kitties below. By clicking a kitty, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.


Each time you win or lose, the numbers that the cats increment by will change.


You win your game by matching the number generated by the computer. You will lose a game if your number goes above that value.


The value of each kitty is hidden until the first time you click on it. If you are playing on hard mode, this value will still not display.


Each time the game starts, the value of the crystals will be redefined.
